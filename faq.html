FAQ

What is Zonkatron™?

Zonkatron is a web app that allows individuals to find their worth and share it with prospective employers. Zonkatron reduces hiring churn by allowing hiring managers to quickly sort through applicants based on their worth.

Why Zonkatron?

We believe that people often don't realize their potential. The mission of Zonkatron is to provide tools to help people uncover their true potential.

At the same time, we believe that companies spend a lot of resources running candidates through their hiring process only to find out that they don't fit. Additionally, many qualified candidates get unnoticed. Zonkatron's goal is to reduce this churn by helping hiring managers make quick decision based on candidate's worth.

Who is it for?

Zonkatron targets individuals and hiring managers. Individuals can find their worth and share it with prospective employers. Hiring managers can quickly pick top candidates based on their worth.

How does it work?

Individuals upload their resume, find their worth and then share it with prospective employers. Hiring managers upload one or more resumes, receive worth for each candidate and then contact top candidates.

What do you mean by worth?

Candidate's worth, or economic value, represents the projected value that a business can gain from a candidate over a particular duration. Worth is expressed in either currency or in % of the maximum possible worth (Worth Index).

How much does it cost?

Zonkatron is free for companies and individuals.

Yes, it is safe. Uploaded resumes never get shared with anyone but the original author. Please read our privacy for more details.
